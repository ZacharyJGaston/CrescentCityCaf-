About Us

Chef Darrell Johnson's Backstory

Chef Darrell Johnson is a New Orleans-native and owner of Crescent City Bistro. The Creole/Cajun restaurant opened in Shreveport of 2016 and relocated to Bossier City in March 2017. Last summer, Johnson appeared on Food Network's highly-intense and hilarious cooking competition, "Cutthroat Kitchen." It was then up to him to out maneuver his three opponents as odd sabotages and limitations were placed in their way as they attempted to make delicious meals each round.
